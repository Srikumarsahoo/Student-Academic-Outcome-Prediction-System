# Research Guide

The research page is structured around the major phases of academic outcome modeling:

- Exploratory data analysis
- Descriptive statistics
- Feature engineering
- PCA inspection
- Model comparison
- Learning curves
- Validation curves
- Confusion matrices
- Feature importance
- Findings, lessons learned, and future work

When exporting notebook figures, place images under `app/static/img/research/` and extend `research.html` to render them.
