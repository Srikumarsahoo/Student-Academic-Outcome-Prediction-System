About

Production-Quality ML Portfolio Project

This application predicts academic outcomes while demonstrating clean architecture, model serving, research communication, validation, analytics, and deployment readiness.

Project Overview

The system helps students, researchers, and academic stakeholders explore whether a student is likely to graduate, drop out, or remain enrolled. It is designed as a modern SaaS-style web application rather than a notebook-only demonstration.

Dataset & Workflow

The workflow includes preprocessing, feature engineering, trained scikit-learn models, probability explanation, anonymous prediction logging, and dashboard aggregation — all served through a production-grade FastAPI backend.

Future Improvements

Planned enhancements include SHAP explanations, calibrated probabilities, additional models such as Gradient Boosting or XGBoost, richer research visualizations, and role-based analytics dashboards.
